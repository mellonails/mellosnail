About the Press-ons

Each set is shaped, painted, and sealed by hand for a lightweight, salon-quality finish. Nails are built with durable gel and high-gloss top coats, then buffed to create a smooth, wearable edge.

Every order includes a prep kit with a mini file, buffer, alcohol prep pads, and adhesive tabs or glue, depending on the requested wear time.

Instructions

  1. Wash hands and dry completely. Push back cuticles and lightly buff the nail surface.
  2. Clean each nail with the alcohol prep pad to remove oils.
  3. Match each press-on to your nail size before applying glue or tabs.
  4. Press firmly for 20-30 seconds starting at the cuticle and rolling forward.
  5. Avoid water for at least one hour after application for maximum hold.
